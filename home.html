Our Mission at CrowdDrive

In today’s hyper-connected world, geographic boundaries no longer limit the potential of vibrant communities. CrowdDrive leverages this connectivity to foster a sustainable and impactful crowdfunding ecosystem in Northwest Pennsylvania. Our platform not only connects innovators with backers but also supports the growth of emerging ventures. By guiding start-ups through crowd-funded projects, we provide a vital source of pre-seed funding and proof-of-concept. This support accelerates their development and strengthens the entrepreneurial landscape of our region.

About Us

CrowdDrive is a collaborative service offering between the Erie Technology Incubator (ETI) and the Dahlkemper School of Business at Gannon University. Our goal is to provide viable pre-seed financing through crowdfunding for start-up companies and entrepreneurs in Northwestern Pennsylvania.

Creators are the start-up companies and entrepreneurs in Northwestern Pennsylvania with unique value propositions. We help them:

  • • Analyze their business and ideas to determine their ability to complete a successful crowdfunding campaign.
  • • Recommend the most appropriate crowdfunding source.
  • • Plan, develop, and manage crowdfunding projects.
  • • Develop launch events.
  • • Connect creators with our donor base.

Donors are true believers of our startup community, whether they reside in Northwestern Pennsylvania or not. We work with them to:

  • • Determine their interests to match them with creators.
  • • Promote the story of the entrepreneurial community in Northwest Pennsylvania.
  • • Hold events for donors and potential donors.
  • • Create leaders and influencers among donors.
  • • Cultivate donors through ongoing engagement.

CrowdDrive is funded through the Erie Community Foundation to establish the CrowdDrive infrastructure and business model. Creators who engage with CrowdDrive to develop and manage crowdfunding campaigns are responsible for $1,500 entry fee for the campaign, 5% of project revenue and 25% of the stretch amount.

Our Recent Projects

At CrowdDrive, we believe in the power of collective effort to bring innovative ideas to life. Our latest projects exemplify this belief, showcasing the transformative impact of community-driven crowdfunding. By partnering with local visionaries in Northwest Pennsylvania, we’ve helped bridge the gap between groundbreaking concepts and tangible success. These projects not only received the crucial pre-seed funding they needed but also gained invaluable market validation, setting the stage for further growth.

Erie, PA

The Erie Technology Incubator serves Northwest Pennsylvania with a unique blend of strategic guidance, dedicated mentor teams, 1:1 coaching, and networking opportunities. Erie Community Foundation

Erie, PA

CrowdDrive is funded through an Erie Community Foundation Helping Today grant. The Erie Community Foundation is a collection of 800 charitable endowments operating under the administrative umbrella of a single public charity.
