Key Players

The Erie Technology Incubator serves Northwest Pennsylvania with a unique blend of strategic guidance, dedicated mentor teams, 1:1 coaching, and networking opportunities. It provides early-stage technology-enabled companies with critical resources and support to guide them through key milestones and phases of their entrepreneurial journey.

The Dahlkemper School of Business at Gannon University serves as a center of ethical business leadership and innovation to provide an excellent education in all areas of business from a values-centered and global perspective and to develop leaders to transform the world of business.

CrowdDrive is funded through an Erie Community Foundation Helping Today grant. The Erie Community Foundation is a collection of 800 charitable endowments operating under the administrative umbrella of a single public charity. The Foundation’s mission is to inspire, engage, and empower donors and communities across the region, today and tomorrow.
